Chicken Pepperoni

Pepperoni pizza is a classic pizza variety that features a thin or thick crust topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and slices of pepperoni. Pepperoni is a type of cured sausage made from pork and beef, seasoned with paprika or chili pepper for a slightly spicy flavor. When baked, the pepperoni slices become crispy around the edges and release their savory oils, adding a deliciously smoky and spicy kick to the pizza. It's one of the most popular pizza choices worldwide, known for its satisfying blend of gooey cheese, tangy tomato sauce, and zesty pepperoni.

Chicken Pepperoni

Chicken pepperoni is a variation of traditional pepperoni made with chicken instead of pork or beef. It typically consists of thinly sliced chicken meat that has been seasoned with spices such as paprika, chili powder, garlic, and pepper, giving it a similar flavor profile to traditional pepperoni. Chicken pepperoni is often used as a topping for pizzas, sandwiches, salads, and pasta dishes, offering a leaner and lighter alternative to traditional pork or beef pepperoni. It retains the familiar smoky, slightly spicy taste associated with pepperoni while catering to those who prefer poultry over red meat or pork.
